HENRY SY


Henry Sy Sr. Hall houses De La Salle University's 14-story library. It is named after the generous benefactor, the late Mr. Henry Sy Sr.

GOKONGWEI


Gokongwei Hall is DLSU's college of Engineering, and it also contains several laboratories for computer science.

VELASCO


Velasco Hall features plenty of laboratories and classrooms that foster innovative learning.

BR. ANDREW


Br. Andrew Gonzalez is the tallest higher education building in the Philippines, boasting 21 stories.

RAZON'S


Enrique Razon Sports Complex contains an olympic-sized pool, basketball courts, and various gyms and classrooms.

ST. LA SALLE


St. La Salle Hall is the university's oldest building and it houses the College of Business and Economics.

ST. MIGUEL


St. Miguel is De La Salle University's College of Liberal Arts.
